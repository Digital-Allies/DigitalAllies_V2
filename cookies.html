What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device to help the site provide a better user experience. In general, cookies are used to retain user preferences, store information for things like language selection, and provide anonymized tracking data to understand how visitors use the site.

How to Disable Cookies

You may prefer to disable cookies on this site and on others. The most effective way to do this is to disable cookies in your browser. I suggest consulting the Help section of your browser or visiting allaboutcookies.org for guidance.
