Accessibility Audits

Identify barriers and provide clear fixes so more people can use your site.

What we check

  • Keyboard navigation and focus order
  • Color contrast and readable type
  • Alt text and media captions
  • Forms, errors, and labels
  • Screen reader behavior

Deliverables

  • Findings organized by severity
  • Remediation plan with examples
  • Quick wins and phased improvements
  • Optional retest to confirm fixes

Standards

  • WCAG 2.1 AA guidance
  • Inclusive content practices
