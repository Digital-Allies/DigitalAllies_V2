Accessibility Audits FAQs

What standards do you use?

  • WCAG 2.1 AA as a baseline.

Do you test with assistive tech?

  • We simulate common scenarios and can arrange expert testing.

How are issues prioritized?

  • By severity, impact, and effort.

Will you fix the issues?

  • We can remediate or guide your developers.

How long does an audit take?

  • Typically 1–2 weeks depending on site size.

Do you provide retesting?

  • Yes. Optional retest to confirm fixes.

Can you train our team?

  • Yes. Short training on patterns and content.

Will this affect design?

  • We offer accessible alternatives that keep your brand.

What do you need to start?

  • URLs, user roles, and key flows to prioritize.

Do you provide documentation?

  • Yes. Clear report with examples.
